Tips for Reducing Waste

Use Reusable Bags

Bring your own reusable bags when shopping to reduce plastic bag waste.

Avoid Single-Use Items

Use reusable water bottles, coffee cups, and food containers.

Compost Food Scraps

Convert food waste into nutrient-rich soil instead of sending it to landfills.

Donate Usable Items

Instead of throwing away clothes, furniture, or electronics, donate them to someone who can use them.

Choose Minimal Packaging

Select products with less packaging or packaging that can be easily recycled.

Repair Before Replacing

Try to fix broken items rather than immediately replacing them with new ones.
